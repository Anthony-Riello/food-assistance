Welcome to Food Assistance

HCI Project By: Esther Chang and Anthony Riello

We were tasked to create a Human Computer Interaction project that is targeted on a specific group or population. Our project aims to address food insecurity by connecting businesses with surplus food to individuals and families in need. We made sure our design was intuitive as possible while keeping our signature green aesthetic to accomodate users of all levels of experience with technology. This was all created and designed in the months of January, February, March, and April of 2025.
