Project Overview

Problem we are Addressing

The problem we will be designing a solution for is low income individuals struggling to afford healthy and nutritional foods.

Goals

  • Make it easy for users to find and access affordable or free food
  • Make it easier for people to sign up as volunteers or share food within their community
  • Help food businesses/restaurants reduce waste from throwing away surplus food

Target Users

  • Low income individuals struggling to afford nutritional food comfortably

Important Stakeholders

Food businesses & restaurant owners

  • Goal: Reduce food waste while supporting the low-income community.
  • Focus: Businesses in high-poverty neighborhoods.
