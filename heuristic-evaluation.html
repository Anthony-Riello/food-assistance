Heuristic Violations

Evaluated By: ResQue

Based on Nielsen’s 10 Usability Heuristics, we identified several areas where the interface could be improved. The number in parentheses indicates the severity of the violation.

  • Visibility of System Status (1): They mentioned that they would like to see some timestamps of when messages are given back and forth for the user’s and vendor’s future references. They also mentioned that it would be nice to alert the user if the vendor is in the chat or not like the typical customer service chat bots.
  • Match Between System and the Real World (2): We didn’t implement an option for different languages.
  • Recognition Rather Than Recall (2): Event details for volunteers
  • Aesthetic and Minimalist Design (2)
  • Help Users Recognize, Diagnose, and Recover from Errors (4)
  • Help and Documentation (3)
